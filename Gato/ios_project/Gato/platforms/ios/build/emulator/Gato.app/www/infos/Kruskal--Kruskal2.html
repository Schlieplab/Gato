Minimal Spanning Tree - Kruskal Trace

This algorithm computes a minimal spanning tree in an undirected graph.

Visualization

Vertex Colors
    
Vertex (and edge) colors designate the connected components in the subgraph induced by the trees during the computation. Therefore, each vertex has its own colors initially.
Edge Colors
    
Initial color for edges. Edges of this color have not been considered for inclusion in the MST yet.
