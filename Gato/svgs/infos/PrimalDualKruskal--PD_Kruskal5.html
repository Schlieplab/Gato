Minimal Spanning Tree - Kruskal (Primal / Dual)

This algorithm finds a minimal spanning tree. Edges in the input are disregarded. Vertices in the input are interpreted as points in the Euclidean plane and the resulting MST can contain edges not present in the input.

Visualization (Main Graph)

Moat Colors
    
Moats are graphical representations of dual variables for individual vertices or groups of vertices. The growing of moats visualizes increases in the value of the corresponding dual variables.
Edge Colors
    
Edges in this color are part of the MST.

Visualization (MST)

Vertex Colors
    
Vertex (and edge) colors designate the connected components in the subgraph induced by the trees during the computation. Therefore, each vertex has its own colors initially.
Edge Colors
    
Edges in the (partial) MST; color indicates the connected component.
