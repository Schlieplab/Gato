This algorithm finds a minimum cost flow by canceling negative cycles. Initially, adding an artificial source-sink-pair and using a maximum flow algorithm, a feasible flow is computed. In the following we search for cycles of negative weight in the residual network, and decrease the cost by sending flow along these cycles, see Chapter 7 of the book.
