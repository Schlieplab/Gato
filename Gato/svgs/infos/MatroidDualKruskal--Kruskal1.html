This algorithm finds a minimal spanning tree in an undirected graph using the dual approach to Kruskals algorithm.

Visualization

Vertex Colors
    
The spanning subgraph in the input which will be the MST upon termination of the algorithm. Initially, all the vertices and edges are part of the spanning subgraph.
Edge Colors
    
Edges which are part of the spanning subgraph.
    
Color of the edge being considered for removal from the spanning subgraph. (transient)
    
Color of edges which have been removed from the spanning subgraph.
